Project details

I was hired to redesign and reimplement the service-booking form from ground up, to convert more visitors into actual service bookings, focusing on making the process smooth on mobile devices.

The old sign-up flow was a very traditional, fairly unstyled, corparate looking, and way too many form fields, that I initially had to convince the client to optimize. My idea was to challange the existing somewhat conservative and restricted CI, while maintaining a calm and professional look/feel. Everything was build with a Jekyll/Bootstrap prototype with a trace of JS.
